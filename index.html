About

GRETE HENRIETTE is a London-based queer artist and designer specialising in handcrafted, ready-to-wear fashion and jewelry. Each piece is meticulously handmade, combining refined metalwork, chainmail, and beadwork with carefully selected natural materials. The name "Grete" means "pearl" in Slavic origin, symbolizing beauty, elegance, and rarity - values that are core to her brand. Influenced by architecture and historical craftsmanship, her designs incorporate freshwater pearls, semi-precious stones, and crystals, defined by precision, durability, and a considered approach to modern luxury.
