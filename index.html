Bespoke

To place a custom order for a signature style jewelry dress, bridal piece, or other bespoke design, please contact info@grete-henriette.com for a quote.

A 50% deposit is required to confirm your order and initiate the design process. The remaining 50% balance is due upon completion, prior to delivery.

Once your order is confirmed, a private consultation call will be arranged with Grete, the designer, to discuss your vision, including silhouette, colour palette, and design preferences. Following this consultation, a series of bespoke sketches will be created for your review. These will be refined in collaboration with you until the final design and colour selection are approved.

Production timelines vary depending on the piece. A classic jewellery dress typically requires 3-4 weeks of dedicated craftsmanship. Bridal designs may take 3-6 weeks, depending on complexity and specifications.

All jewellery dresses are handcrafted in our Atelier in Hackney, London, and shipped worldwide via tracked UPS delivery.

About

GRETE HENRIETTE is a London-based queer artist and designer celebrated for her visionary approach to fashion, combining intricate metalwork and beadwork in sculptural garments, both couture and ready-to-wear. Inspired by historical craftsmanship, architecture, and the raw forces of nature, her work explores themes of strength, protection, restriction and transformation. The name "Grete" means "pearl" in Slavic origin, symbolising beauty, elegance, and rarity, values at the core of her brand.

Grete has dressed the likes of Alicia Keys, Paris Hilton, Tyla, Addison Rey, Zara Larsson, BLACKPINK, TEMS, Sam Smith, Lizzo, Lil Nas X, Tinashe, Rebecca Black, Mia Khalifa, Saweetie, Kim Petras, Jorja Smith, Halle Bailey, Sabrina Sato, Sabrina Claudio, Toni Garrn, Paloma Elsesser, Mona Tougard and many more. Grete has been featured in magazines such as VOGUE, W, CR FASHION BOOK, SHOWStudio, PERFECT, GQ, ELLE, DAZED, COSMOPOLITAN, METAL, and many more.
